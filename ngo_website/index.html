About NayePankh Foundation

NayePankh Foundation was born during the challenging days of the COVID-19 pandemic when a group of young individuals came together with a shared goal of helping those in need. What began as a small effort to support underprivileged communities gradually evolved into a large youth-led movement dedicated to creating meaningful social impact.

Today, NayePankh Foundation works across multiple areas including education, food distribution, community welfare, menstrual hygiene awareness, and support for underprivileged families. Driven by the belief that every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope, the organization continues to empower communities and inspire positive change through collective action.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities by providing access to education, essential resources, and opportunities for growth. We strive to address social challenges at the grassroots level and encourage young people to become active contributors to nation building.

Through compassion, innovation, and community participation, we aim to create sustainable impact and empower individuals to build brighter futures for themselves and their families.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every individual has access to opportunities, resources, and support necessary to live a dignified life. Through youth leadership and community-driven initiatives, we aim to create a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered India.

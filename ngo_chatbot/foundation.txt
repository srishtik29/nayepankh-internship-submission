NayePankh Foundation

About:
NayePankh Foundation is one of India's largest student-led NGOs.
It was founded during the COVID-19 pandemic to help communities in need.

Mission:
To empower individuals through education, awareness, and social initiatives.

Programs:
- Education Support
- Food Distribution
- Menstrual Hygiene Awareness
- Clothing Drives

Volunteer:
Students can volunteer in education, social media, outreach, content creation, and event management.

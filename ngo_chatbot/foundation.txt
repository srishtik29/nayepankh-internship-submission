NayePankh Foundation

About:
NayePankh Foundation is one of India's largest student-led NGOs. It was established during the COVID-19 pandemic with the aim of helping communities through education, social welfare, and youth-driven initiatives.

Mission:
To empower individuals through education, awareness, and community support.

Vision:
To create a society where every individual has access to opportunities and resources needed for growth and development.

Programs:
- Education Support
- Food Distribution
- Menstrual Hygiene Awareness
- Clothing Drives
- Community Welfare Initiatives

Volunteer Opportunities:
Students can contribute through teaching, content creation, social media management, awareness campaigns, outreach programs, and event management.
